# NPC Villages
## Prosperity
- Villages increase NPC quality by shifting to more villagers with 2-3 stars and less low/no star NPCs.
- Also important to increase head count of an NPC village by building houses there
- To increase prosperity it’s normally sufficient to just sell a town the things that are red in their economy overview tab. So you don’t necessarily buy stuff from them.
- Increasing prosperity by 1 gives a village one more carts [info from discord chat not verified]. At prosperity 1 they start with 1. With a cart they can buy from other villages or from one of your markets [needs verification]
