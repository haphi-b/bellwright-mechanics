# Player Settlements
- Building structures adds these buildings to an existing settlement. Somehow all buildings also form a settlement area. This is the relevant area for guarding AND idle workers that take a stroll around. So when adding a new structure that is actually far away from your settlement that new structure extends your existing settlement to that other structure. Guards might walk all the way over to the new building
- Normally workers will automatically bring building materials and start constructing structures of the same settlement they belong to but not the structures of other settlements. Generally more mechanics strictly separate between different settlements: storage, sleeping quarters/houses and working jobs.
- Travel signs that are not in your settlement and far away should better be in their own settlement. Players normally have a “private” or “travelsigns” settlement for this. You can also create a private storage that only you can access by assigning a storage building within your settlement to some pseudo settlement (like “private”).
