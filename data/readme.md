Place raw data from modkit, experiments or testing results into this folder.

Don't place 'proof' pictures or sth. because it would go beyond scope. Please just keep it higher level. Not an interpretation but only the observations you did.

During a PR review it would however make sense to manually provide a reviewer with additional evidence to prove your data.

We are humans. We believe in our hypothesis and we always should believe we are right. Experience shows that it's just natural that one is so convinced they'd make numbers up, because testing is quite time consuming and not fun. It's okay, it's normal. For that we have a review.
